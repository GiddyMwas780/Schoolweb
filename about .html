WHICH STUDENTS ARE ADMITTED??

Maai-Mahiu boys is a good extra-county school and as a result evrey year we admit students about 300-400 which had passed well in their primary school with the avarage of 320 marks in their KCPE exams.Our school admits students from all parts of the country without discrimination or corruption provided the students meet the qualifications. The school urges the young boys in high school who would wish to join us to read and work hard tirelessly to ensure they get an opportunity to join us and also enjoy the fruits of being part of Maai-Mahiu boys the school for the ROYALS


Our performance

The school is one of the good performing school around Naivasha sub-county and always ranked as among the top five and not only in Naivasha sub-county because it also performs well in the Nakuru county.Over the past years the school has been taking a good number of students to the university and through the effort put on we are hoping to take larger numbers to the universities.
