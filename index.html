Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

How do I enroll my child in the school?

To enroll your child, please visit our admissions office or apply online through our website.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a variety of extracurricular activities including sports, music, drama, and clubs.

How can I contact the school office?

You can contact the school office via phone at +254 722811303 or maaimahiuboys@gmail.com

What is the school's homework policy?

Our homework policy is designed to reinforce classroom learning. Homework is assigned daily and varies by grade level.

Are meals provided at the school?

Yes, we provide breakfast and lunch for all students. Our menu is available on the school website.

What support services are available for students?

We offer support services including counseling, special education, and tutoring.

How can I get involved in the school community?

Parents can get involved by joining the PTA, volunteering, or attending school events.
